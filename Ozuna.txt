His aim is to write positive ,uplifting songs that encourage people to work toward their dreams, and he avoids using profanity out of respect for his daughter. a songwriter since the age of 12, he signed a record contract in 2014 and began posting songs to Youtube. by 2015 songs such as &quot;Si tu marido no te Quiere&quot; had become radio hits across latin america, and he performed concerts in numerous countries. In march of 2016 he released a remix of his song &quot;No quiere Enamoarase&quot; which featured superstar Daddy Yankee: the song immediately racked up millions of Youtube views in 2017, he delivered his full-length debut album, &quot;Odisea&quot; which featured guest appearances by De La Ghetto, Anuel AA, J balvin and others included on the album were the singles &quot;Tell Him That You Love Him&quot;, &quot;Your Photo&quot;, and &quot;A Flower&quot; in september, the album peaked at a number one on the Top Latin Albums Chart.</p>
